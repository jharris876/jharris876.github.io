About Me

Bachelor of Computer Science

Hello! My name is Jacob Harris. I am a recent graduate from Tennessee Technological University. In school I was able to work in a fast paced and team oriented environment. In my senior year my team and I worked through 6 iterations using the SCRUM development process to create a master application for the TANF(Temporary Assistance for Needy Families) Opportunity Act. As a team we were given backend access to research multiple government applications to reduce redundancies for applicants. Every week we met with our customer to ensure we met their needs through out our development process. At the end of the project we derived a master application that is being used by a vendor to develop a full application without redundancies for TANF Act applicants. I am very passionate about making organized, quality work and I strive to make clean and readable code with any project. I can easily communicate with anyone on a team and be a mentor when I can. When I'm not working I enjoy playing video games, working out, playing basketball and spending time with friends.
